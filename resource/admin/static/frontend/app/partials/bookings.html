Booking Procedure

  1. Download the current bookings and dlab usage policy below
  2. Read through both documents
  3. email supervisor@eie.wits.ac.za with the details of the booking Bookings will be confirmed by email.
  4. Confirm with the student lab administrators that:
    1. you will have the full complement of systems which you book
    2. the number of systems you will need
    3. your required software is installed
  5. an assistant administrator will be on standby for the booking.
  6. Inform the PG demis that the wheelchair access door must be closed unless a demi is posted at the door – sounds unreasonable, but there are valid reasons for insisting that the door be either closed or access be controlled by a demi. Soneone may close the door if you leave the door open!
